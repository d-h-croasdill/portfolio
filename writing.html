Twine Cookbook, 2022


I was the copyeditor for a currently unreleased edition of the Twine Cookbook, a common-language documentation of the Twine 2 game engine. On behalf of the Interactive Fiction Technology Foundation, I helped prepare the current copy of the cookbook for print publication. This included doing a proofread, clarity/consistency edit, & checking the provided example code in all 4 of Twine's coding languages.

Aporias, 2022


Aporias is a tabletop roleplaying system & supplement, featuring 120+ pages of adventure, hijinks, & story prompts. Completed as my thesis project for the MFA program at the NYU Game Center, it's a blend of technical & fiction writing. I took a more playful approach to the rules writing, which worked well to incentivize more engaged reading on the player's part.

Advanced Checkers 2, 2022

Advanced Checkers 2 is the latest incarnation of my much-beloved student project Checkers 2. While the game is currently in-development, AC2's express edition completely playable due to my mastery of an ancient game design technique: legible flowcharts.
