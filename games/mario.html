play mario
2022, platformer
made as a gift for mut moochi
solo project
tools used: Twine 2, Photoshop, Miro

play mario was made a christmas gift for my friend named mut. In play mario, you move the famous plumber man around several scenes by clicking & dragging. You have to make Mario do all of the jumps by moving his icon around, & you progress through the game by reporting your progress on the right side of the screen.
