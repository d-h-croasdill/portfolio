Hey there.
If you were somehow unaware, I'm d.h. croasdill, award-winning game designer, wiley lesbian, & master of all four fine arts. I've been making games for four years, & writing extraordinary works for eight.

Given that my work consistently returns to the material of language & the act of creating narrative, one would be tempted to think that I'm one of those "writers" one hears about on the news. But one would be wrong-- I actually never learned how to spell. C'est la vie.

I'm currently based out of Northampton, MA.
