I was born in a city in Michigan. Then I lived at the bottom of the Puget Sound. Now I live in the Big City, the Big City which everyone knows & loves. Truly mine is a rich tapestry of experience.

I used to wake up with mysterious, rust-colored stains on my hands. I was sure I was cursed. My pants are all stained below the knees & also above the knees. I am always saying two things at once & everybody loves this. Everybody loves that I can't talk straight. I'm still afraid of magical spells, but that's my choice.

I have a BA in Freud & poetry. I have an MFA in game design & being tired for years. Despite my charms, I have had three polemical essays written about my work, & can claim a few ornery bullet points as well. I was honorably mentioned at IGF & won some awards in high school for being eccentric. I'm extremely employable. I'm not bragging, it's just the truth. You should give it a shot.
